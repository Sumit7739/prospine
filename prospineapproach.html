Equipment We Use

Robospine 100: Gentle Spinal Decompression

The Robospine 100 provides smooth, effective spinal decompression, ideal for patients with disc issues who prefer a comfortable, non-invasive treatment. Its advanced system customizes and adjusts therapy, making it relaxing—even sleep-inducing for some patients. An excellent option for treating chronic spine conditions.

Shockwave Pro - 1000: Advanced Pain Relief

Our clinic uses the Shockwave Pro - 1000 to provide fast, effective relief for muscle knots, tendinitis, and tissue adhesions. This advanced shockwave therapy treats pain in just 1-3 sessions, targeting problem areas precisely to restore mobility and reduce discomfort quickly. Ideal for large muscle groups, Shockwave Pro offers non-invasive relief tailored to each patient’s needs.

TTECH LASER SS-2000: Advanced Scanning Laser Therapy

Using the TECH LASER SS-2000, we provide targeted laser therapy for effective pain relief. This advanced system treats multiple trigger points simultaneously, delivering precise doses of red and infrared laser without the need to hold or move the probe.
