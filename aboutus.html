The Pro Spine Story

Back pain affects 8 out of 10 individuals, yet only 5% of these cases require surgical intervention thanks to advancements in medical technology. In India, however, over 300,000 surgeries are performed annually, many of which do not address the underlying issues, leading to persistent pain and relapses. Founded in 2011, Pro Spine aims to transform back pain management in India. Our innovative treatment methods and multidisciplinary approach have enabled countless patients to avoid unnecessary surgeries. We collaborate closely with an advisory panel of leading spine surgeons, neurologists, rheumatologists, and doctors of physical medicine, both in India and internationally, to provide comprehensive care that targets the root cause of your pain.


About Pro Spine

Pro Spine is a state-of-the-art physiotherapy clinic dedicated to spinal and musculoskeletal health. Our mission is to provide advanced, non-surgical solutions that empower our patients to live pain-free, active lives. With a focus on cutting-edge therapies, Pro Spine helps individuals achieve better spinal health, manage pain effectively, and, when possible, avoid surgical interventions.

Specialized Services

At Pro Spine, we offer a range of specialized therapies tailored for optimal spinal and musculoskeletal health, including robotic spinal decompression and advanced pelvic floor rehabilitation. These state-of-the-art treatments target conditions with precision, providing relief, improving mobility, and supporting patients in avoiding invasive surgery whenever possible. With our unique offerings, Pro Spine stands out as a center of excellence in spine care.

Expert Team

Our clinic is led by Dr. Pranav Kumar, an award-winning physical therapist certified in pelvic floor therapy, with a focus on spinal health and pain management. Dr. Kumar's expertise and dedication to innovative therapies ensure each patient receives exceptional care. Supported by a skilled team, Pro Spine is equipped to provide top-quality, personalized treatments, helping patients achieve sustainable recovery and improved quality of life.

Personalized Rehabilitation Program

At Pro Spine, we understand that each patient's journey to recovery is unique. Our Personalized Rehabilitation Program is designed to minimize pain and disability while helping patients achieve their desired health goals and prevent the recurrence of pain.

Each treatment plan is created based on an algorithm tailored specifically to the individual’s condition. Our knowledge management system draws on data intelligence accumulated from treating over 90,000 patients, allowing us to assign each case to one of over 100 different conditions. This ensures that your treatment plan is backed by proven effectiveness for others with similar conditions.

Our goal is to provide you with a comprehensive and effective rehabilitation experience, empowering you to take charge of your health and achieve lasting wellness.
